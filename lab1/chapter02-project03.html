Mademoiselle Caroline Rivière

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The portrait of Mademoiselle Caroline was painted in 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Dominique Ingres, and today hangs in the Louvre. It is the third of three portraits of the Rivière family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Dimension: 100 x 70cm

Home: Musèe du Louvre, Paris
